About me

My name is Ángel Larrañaga Muro. I have been released on June of 1992 in Madrid (Spain), where I currently live.
For as long as I can remember, I have been blamed for disassembling anything that crossed my path.
I like to learn about how things work and how they are made. I also love to create things. I am an OpenSource Maker, I think that it is important to share the knowledge and guarantee its access for everyone, everywhere. That is why I like to use OpenSource tools to develop my projects.
Robotics and electronics are my passions and I usually have more pending projects than time to work on them.
If you want to see my projects or know more about me, do not hesitate to click on the links above!
