About me

Hello! My name is Ángel Larrañaga Muro. I have been released on June of 1992 in Madrid (Spain), where I actually live.
As long as I can remember, I have been blamed for disassembly everything that crossed in my path.
I like to learn about how things work and how are they made. I love to create things too. I'm a maker, an OpenSource Maker. I think that it's important to share the knowledge and guarantee its access for everyone, everywhere. That's why I like to use OpenSource tools!
Robotics and electronics are my passions and I use to have more pending projects than time. I enjoy Science Fiction movies and books too!
If you want to see about my projects or know more about me, don't hesitate to click on any of the links above.
